Strikle

On December 8, 2022, New York Times workers staged a 24 hour walkout in protest of the company's failure to bargain in good faith with the union. Among the worker's requests for consumers was a boycott of all NYT services, including Wordle. Strikle is a Wordle alternative intended to give Wordle players a chance to play their daily Wordle without crossing the NYT workers' picket line. Strikle was played by thousands of people, and was even featured in a Vice Motherboard article.
